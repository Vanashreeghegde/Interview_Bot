**A smart, interactive, fresher-to-intermediate level Data Science interview simulator**  
Practice real-world interview questions across **Machine Learning, Python, SQL, Statistics, and more** — with **adaptive difficulty**, **live 45-minute timer**, and **instant AI-driven scoring & feedback**.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- 🌟 **Real Interview Simulation:** Questions are scenario-based, mimicking top tech company interviews.  
- 🎛️ **Adaptive Difficulty:** Starts at fresher level and gradually becomes intermediate based on your answers.  
- 🔀 **Randomized Topics:** Covers ML, Python, SQL, Deep Learning, Feature Engineering, Data Cleaning, and more.  
- ⏱️ **Live 45-Minute Timer:** Auto-ends the interview if time runs out.  
- 📝 **Instant Evaluation:** Answers scored immediately (0-10) by AI.  
- 📊 **Final Feedback Report:** Structured insights on strengths, weaknesses, and improvement roadmap.  
- 🔄 **Restart Anytime:** Option to end or restart the interview at will.  

---

## 💻 Tech Stack

- **Python 3.12** – Core backend logic  
- **Streamlit** – Interactive frontend for live questions and timer  
- **Goq API** – AI-powered question generation & evaluation  
- **Hybrid RAG Retrieval** – Ensures contextually accurate, non-hallucinated questions  
- **.env** – Secure API key management
